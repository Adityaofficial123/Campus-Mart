Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I buy a product?

Browse the listings, click on the product you want, and click “Proceed to Payment” to secure your purchase.

❓ How do I confirm I’ve received my product?

After receiving your product, go to your cart (top right) and click the “Confirm” button for that product.

❓ What if I don’t like the product?

If you don’t like the product, we offer a full refund minus a 5% platform fee. Contact our support team to start a return.

❓ How long does delivery take?

Typically, products are delivered within 3-5 business days. We’ll update you via email with tracking details once shipped.

❓ Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel within 2 hours of purchase by contacting our support team.
